# Concept: Generic Ecommerce Chatbot Library

## Vision
To provide a plug-and-play, AI-driven chatbot solution specifically tailored for ecommerce. This library allows businesses to quickly deploy a sophisticated, human-like virtual assistant that understands complex rules, integrates with their existing logic via APIs, and runs optimally on their available hardware.

## Core Philosophy
1.  **Ease of Use**: A smart installer handles the heavy lifting of environment setup and model selection.
2.  **Autonomy & Intelligence**: The chatbot shouldn't just respond to keywords; it should understand business rules and help users complete tasks (reporting issues, checking inventory, etc.).
3.  **Local vs. Cloud Flexibility**: Integration with both local LLMs (Ollama/Llama) and cloud-based options (DeepSeek/GPT) via API keys.
4.  **Local Fine-tuning**: Enable businesses to "train" the chatbot on their specific rules and datasets locally.
5.  **Human-Like Interaction**: Avoid rigid, menu-driven flows. The AI should guide the user, handle missing information gracefully, and maintain a natural conversation.

## Target Use Cases
-   **Customer Support**: Automating responses to common queries, order status checks, and troubleshooting.
-   **Sales Assistant**: Recommending products, providing details on features, and guiding users through the purchase flow.
-   **Issue Tracking**: Reporting and following up on service interruptions or product defects.
